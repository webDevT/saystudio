Meet the team

Matthew Sexton and Laila Al-Yousuf established SAY Studio in 2018 with a clear and simple vision: to provide a truly personalised solution complemented by global expertise. Having both worked for some of the world’s largest design firms, Matthew and Laila bring decades of experience in delivering some of the region’s most iconic projects. The partners are supported by a dynamic team of design experts who love taking on challenges, both big and small, to create exceptional designs and long-lasting relationships.
